About The Artist


The Artist… CR Sadler is a quintessential renaissance cowboy. He is a bison and cattle rancher, a painter to the stars, and a multi generation artesian, who also developed and tended honey bee apiaries in several states – The Sadler Honey Company. A native of Wyoming, USA, he sold his first painting at the age of five. His art was so spectacular Yellowstone National Park created its first art show in his honor. He has won numerous juried art contests including the ‘Wyoming Leter Buck Western Art Show’ and the ‘Cowboy Hall of Fame’. The Grand Gallery New York purchased two collections in the early nineties, and are now residing in the library of the Smithsonian Institute Library. Currently, his oil paintings are selling for over $200,000. His works can be found among colleges, world leaders, museums, and celebrities. Cr Sadler art collectors include notables Ted Turner, Jane Fonda, Oprah Winfrey, Wayne Newton and Sir Charles Cleveland III buyer for the late Dudley Moore.

Global Art Release… Due to requests from friends and art connoisseurs, the Sadler Galleries have graciously released selections below, along with limited edition collections not available to the public since 1981. CR Sadler personally signs and numbers each print and original work. History has proven that even in economic strains and downturns, art remains valuable and will continue to appreciate as well as being a prestigious, educational and historical gift. Owning a CR Sadler work is owning a piece of the Old West, your own piece of history directly from CR Sadler to you.
